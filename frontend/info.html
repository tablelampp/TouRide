About

Most tourist in Canada are given access to a license without understanding the rules of driving in Canada often leading to confusion when driving. We offer a comprehensive guide for tourists to master the essential driving rules and regulations in Canada. Our goal is to ensure you feel confident and secure behind the wheel. Proudly accredited by the Ministry of Transportation, our guide is your trusted resource for safe and informed driving.

In 2023, approximately 27.2 million international visitors travelled to Canada

The course provides a formal guide to the driving laws in Canada to help you feel safe when driving

By completing the course and recieving the certificate approved by the Ministry of Transportation, you can potentially reduce the price of insurance for your car!
